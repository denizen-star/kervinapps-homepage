Overview

Community Builder App is an AI-powered, hyper-local networking platform that creates meaningful in-person connections within professional hubs. Unlike traditional networking apps, CBA focuses on facilitating real-world meetings and community building.

Using intelligent matching algorithms and location-based features, the app helps professionals discover and connect with like-minded individuals in their immediate vicinity.
