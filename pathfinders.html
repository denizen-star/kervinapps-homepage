Pathfinders

Professional networking data collection platform

TypeScript Live

Overview

Pathfinders is an intelligent platform that connects people through demographic data analysis, fostering meaningful professional relationships based on shared backgrounds and interests. The application streamlines the process of collecting and organizing professional contact information at networking events.

Built with modern web technologies, Pathfinders offers a clean, intuitive interface that makes data collection effortless while maintaining professional standards and data privacy.
