Design System Specification v1.0

Design Principles

1. Strava's Activity-First Layout
Event scheduling prioritizes date and time visibility. Brilliant orange accent creates energy and urgency, perfect for motivating group participation and active engagement.
2. Meetup's Community Focus
Social elements are prominent: attendee counts, group messages, and shared routes. Information architecture emphasizes connection and collective activity.
