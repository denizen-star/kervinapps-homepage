Design System Specification v1.0

Design Principles

1. Linear's Status-Driven Design
Clear visual states for application tracking. Hunter uses AI to match candidates to jobs with smart document generation. Orange accent drives attention to action items and high-match opportunities.
2. Notion's Information Architecture
Job applications are organized with AI-generated summaries, match scores, and automated document creation. Hierarchy ensures rapid assessment of application quality and fit.
