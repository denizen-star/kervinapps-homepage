Pathfinders

Design System Specification v1.0

Design Principles

1. Figma's Navigation Clarity
Clear visual pathways guide users through complex journeys. Cyan accent serves as a wayfinding element, creating visual breadcrumbs throughout the interface.
2. Trello's Progressive Disclosure
Information is revealed progressively as users advance through their path. Each stage is clearly delineated with status indicators and contextual information.
