Optimizer

Calendar-first habit assistant

TypeScript In Development

Overview

Optimizer is a calendar-first habit assistant that schedules minimum-viable actions into real, available time with evidence-based reviews. Unlike traditional habit trackers, Optimizer integrates directly with your calendar to ensure habits fit into your actual schedule.

The application uses smart algorithms to find optimal times for your habits based on your calendar availability, energy levels, and past performance data.
