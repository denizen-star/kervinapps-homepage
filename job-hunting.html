Hunter

AI-powered job search assistant

Python In Development

Overview

Hunter is an AI-powered job search assistant that analyzes job descriptions, generates customized resumes and cover letters, and provides intelligent match scoring using local AI. Everything runs on your computer for complete privacy.

With Hunter, you can apply to 10 jobs in the time it used to take for 1. It automatically creates tailored documents, tracks your applications, and helps you focus on the jobs where you have the best chance of success.

Key Features

Smart Job Matching

AI analyzes job descriptions and calculates your match score (0-100%)

Automatic Document Generation

Creates customized cover letters, tailored resumes, and qualification analyses

Application Tracking Dashboard

Visual dashboard with status tracking and match scores for all applications

Technology Skills Matching

Visual breakdown showing matched, partial, and missing skills

Hiring Team Intelligence

Extracts hiring team info and LinkedIn connection data

Local AI Privacy

100% private - runs locally using Ollama, no cloud services
