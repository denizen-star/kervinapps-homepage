Overview

Hunter is an AI-powered job search assistant that analyzes job descriptions, generates customized resumes and cover letters, and provides intelligent match scoring using local AI. Everything runs on your computer for complete privacy.

With Hunter, you can apply to 10 jobs in the time it used to take for 1. It automatically creates tailored documents, tracks your applications, and helps you focus on the jobs where you have the best chance of success.
