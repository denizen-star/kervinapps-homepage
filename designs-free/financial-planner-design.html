Financial Planner

Alternative Design System v2.0

Design Principles

1. Bloomberg Terminal's Data Precision
Monospace typography throughout ensures perfect numerical alignment. Gold accent represents value and achievement, creating aspirational yet professional aesthetic for financial planning.
2. Charles Schwab's Target Approach
Concentric circles represent financial goals and progress toward targets. Clean, geometric visualization removes emotional decision-making from financial data.
