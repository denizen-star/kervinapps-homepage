ALTERNATIVE DESIGNS

Completely Different Approaches - Professional Dev Tool Branding v2.0

Design Philosophy

This collection presents completely different design directions for each application, maintaining professional development tool standards while exploring diverse visual metaphors, color palettes, and typographic treatments.

Each design uses unique iconography: hexagonal patterns, target circles, ladder progression, lightning bolts, compass navigation, stopwatch timing, and city grids. No approach duplicates the original design set.
