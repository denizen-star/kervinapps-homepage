Hunter

Alternative Design System v2.0

Design Principles

1. Asana's Task Hierarchy
Ladder metaphor represents career progression through AI-powered job matching. Hunter analyzes fits, generates documents, and tracks applications. Teal accent conveys professional growth and opportunity assessment.
2. Airtable's Clean Structure
Rounded corners and softer edges create approachable interface. Hunter's AI automation handles resume tailoring and cover letter generation, reducing application anxiety through intelligent organization and match scoring.
