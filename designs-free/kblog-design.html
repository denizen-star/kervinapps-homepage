Design Principles

1. New York Times' Editorial Design
Light mode with serif typography creates traditional publishing aesthetic. Amber accent provides warmth and authority, perfect for thought leadership in data architecture. Clean white background enhances readability.
2. Substack's Focused Reading
Generous line-height and larger type sizes optimize long-form reading. Publication-quality design elevates technical content to professional editorial standards with newspaper-inspired layout principles.
