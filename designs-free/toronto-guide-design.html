Design Principles

1. Citymapper's Urban Navigation
Grid-based map interface represents city block structure. Coral-red accent creates warmth and approachability for city exploration, differentiating from cold tech blues.
2. Airbnb's Locality Focus
Skyline silhouette immediately conveys Toronto identity. Grid markers allow precise location placement. Design balances technical precision with exploratory joy of urban discovery.
