Overview

Toronto Guide is a curated, Swiss-designed concierge service for discovering Toronto's best experiences. With systematic curation and clean UX, it offers a refined approach to exploring the city's restaurants, attractions, and hidden gems.

Inspired by Swiss design principles of clarity and precision, the guide focuses on quality over quantity, presenting carefully vetted recommendations with beautiful, minimalist presentation.

Key Features

Swiss Design Aesthetic

Clean, minimalist interface inspired by Swiss design principles

Curated Experiences

Hand-picked recommendations from local experts

Location-Based Discovery

Find the best spots near you or explore by neighborhood

Quality Over Quantity

Systematic curation ensures only the best makes the list
