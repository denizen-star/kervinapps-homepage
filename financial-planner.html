Overview

Financial Planner is designed to help you plan, track, and optimize your personal finances with clarity and ease using a lightweight, actionable system. No complex spreadsheets or overwhelming features—just the essentials done right.

The app focuses on making financial planning accessible and actionable, with clear visualizations and practical insights that help you make informed decisions about your money.

Key Features

Budget Tracking

Simple, visual budget management with spending insights

Financial Goals

Set and track progress toward your financial objectives

Data Visualization

Clear charts and graphs to understand your financial health

Actionable Insights

Practical recommendations based on your spending patterns
